World Vaccine Status while COVID Pandemic

In the midst of this pandemic, getting vaccinated has not been an uncommon method for many countries all over the world to handle this COVID problem. However, not all countries has high vaccination rate and even some countries has not administered a vaccination program. This visualisation will show illustration of how country status could affect COVID vaccine distribution program in each different country.


GDP

Gross domestic product (GDP) is the market value of all goods and services produced within a country’s borders in a specific time period. In simple terms, GDP is most commonly used measure for the size of an economy of a country.


Life Expectency

Life Expectency refers to the number of years a person can expect to live. By definition, life expectancy is based on an estimate of the average age a person that lives in that certain country will be when they die.


Population Growth

Population growth is the increase in the number of people in a population. Population growth refers to change in the size of a population—which can be either positive or negative—over time, depending on the balance of births and deaths.
